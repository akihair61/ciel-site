Booking & Cancellation

Cancellation & Reschedule

  • If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment will be automatically cancelled.
  • In such cases, 100% of the reserved service fee will be charged as a cancellation fee.
  • Cancellations or changes within 2 days of the appointment are not accepted.
  • If you wish to cancel or reschedule, please contact us at least 2 days in advance.
  • If you cancel or reschedule within 2 days of your appointment, 100% of the reserved service fee will be charged.
  • The cancellation fee will be invoiced to the phone number and email address provided at the time of booking.
  • If payment is not made or communication is ignored, we reserve the right to take appropriate legal action.

We appreciate your understanding and cooperation.
These policies help us provide the best possible service to every guest, and keep our standard at its finest.
