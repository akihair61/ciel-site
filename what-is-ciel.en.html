What is Ciel

Ciel Hair Studio

A design of comfort without borders

Ciel means "sky" in French —
vast, open, and always there.

At Ciel, we honour each person's individuality — beyond language, culture, or values. We believe beauty isn't defined by appearance, but by how you feel in your own skin.

Whether you've come from another country or are simply having a different kind of day, you are welcomed exactly as you are. No pressure, no pretence.

We create quiet moments where the mind settles and your true self gently rises to the surface.

This is a new form of beauty.
This is the design of Ciel.

— Ciel
