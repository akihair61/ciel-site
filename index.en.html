Our Story

Get to know Ciel

Our salon was created not just to style hair, but to provide a calm and relaxing space where you can unwind and feel at ease.

At the heart of Ciel is Aki — the No.1 Japanese colourist in Bangkok. Through careful consultation and a relaxing ambience, we help each guest's natural beauty rise gently to the surface — a place where everyone can truly be themselves.

Founder & Creative Director

Aki
Stylist / Colourist

FAQ

Questions

First visit & language

Do you speak English or Japanese?+

We have Japanese-speaking staff and serve guests in Japanese, English and Thai, so you can discuss everything in your own language.

Can you work with Western / non-Asian hair?+

Yes. We handle both Asian and Western hair types.

Is there a consultation?+

Yes — a thorough one. We sometimes use our own counselling app to fine-tune exactly the look you want.

Booking & cancellation

Do I need a reservation?+

We recommend booking in advance — online via Fresha is the smoothest.

How do I book?+

Via Fresha (recommended), LINE, WhatsApp or phone.

What about cancellations and changes?+

Please read our booking policy before reserving. View booking policy →

Pricing & payment

What payment methods do you accept?+

Cash, QR payment (Thai bank accounts only) and Visa / Mastercard. Card payments incur a 2.5% surcharge.

Are the listed prices final?+

For services other than cuts, prices vary with hair length and volume. The final price is confirmed during your in-salon consultation.

Are there first-time discounts or set menus?+

Yes. Please check the details and prices on Fresha.

Menu & techniques

Can I come for just a cut or fringe trim?+

Yes. We also offer a fringe-only cut.

Can you do a straight perm on bleached hair?+

We're sorry, but we don't perform straightening on bleached or heavily damaged hair (to avoid breakage).

Can I book a head spa only?+

Yes, the head spa is available on its own.

Are men and children welcome?+

Both are welcome (the kids' cut is for children aged 10 and under).

Access & hours

Where are you and how do I get there?+

Ariston Hotel 2F (Sukhumvit Soi 24) — about 100m (a 2-minute walk) from BTS Phrom Phong, right by Emporium and EmQuartier.

What are your opening hours?+

10:00–19:00. Closed every Wednesday and on the 1st & 3rd Tuesday of the month (Tuesday may vary).
